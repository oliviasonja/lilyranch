Support us

We are a small, self-sustaining business. All the proceeds go to keeping both the animals and workers at Lily Ranch safe and healthy. Most of the funds go to housing, food, warmth, and veterinary care. Therefore we always appreciate help in many ways! Here are some ways you can help us:

Donate $20

This is for us to be able to repair the cages for the birds.

Donate $50

Adopt one of our animals for $50. These animals are those who need extra help with their care, such as older animals or those with medical conditions.

Donate $100

Help us pay for veterinary treatment for our animals at the clinic.
