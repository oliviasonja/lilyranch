Where are we located?

Getting to our location can be done either by car or by local transport. Taking the train to the nearest town "Codetown" and bus 135 to our stop, "Lily Ranch", will take you around an hour. Driving from Codetown will take you around fifteen minutes.

Our address is: Lily road 18, 12483 Codetown
